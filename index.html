Our Story



Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

  • First We Meet

    February 9, 2017

    A true Canadian meeting over a quick coffee which turned to two and an evening of talking cut off by our own exhaustion.

  • 2nd Coffee Date

    February 26, 2017

    Meet at Williams Coffee, London where one turned into two and the restaurant closed. We walked Victoria park past the point of cold because we didn't want to leave.

  • First Date

    March 21, 2017

    A day date filled with cards, laughter, endless conversation, coffee and gardens. Mike surprised Hailey and made boterkoek winning her over and earning a first kiss. Sparks flew with the kiss of fairytales!

  • Next Steps

    May 2017

    Our little families meet for the first time. We then began planning many adventures together as a family and enjoyed countless more.

  • The Ring!

    January 6, 2024

    Mike booked a nice BnB in the country where we went to The Artisan Grill for dinner, one of Hailey's favourite places. Back to our private apartment with hot tub, flowers, wine and the perfect proposal. The ring was made special from Hailey's Mim wedding band and a ring from her mom, topping it off with hand selecting a hexagon, premium cut Alexandrite for the setting.

  • More Adventures to Come!

    Finally moving in together, getting marries, navigating our careers, and our epic honeymoon adventure!

    Our story will never end!
